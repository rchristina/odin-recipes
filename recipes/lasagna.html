Lasagna

Description

This dish should be a staple in your recipe book as it is a dish the whole family can enjoy. A classic dish to give you that cozy home cooked vibes. Easy to make and easy to store so you can enjoy later.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Combine cheese filling ingredients in a bowl and set aside
  2. Heat up ground beef
  3. Add marinara sauce and tomato paste
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees farenheit
  5. Boil pot of water on stove
  6. Add lasagna noodles and boil until al dente
  7. Drain and rinse noodles
  8. Add a layer of noodles on a baking sheet
  9. Add a layer of meat sauce next
  10. Add another layer of noodles
  11. Add a layer of cheese filling
  12. Repeat steps 8 through 11
  13. Top with Mozzarella
  14. Bake for 35 minutes
