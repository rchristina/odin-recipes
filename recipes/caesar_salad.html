Caesar Salad

Description

This is the perfect appetizer for any dinner. Little effort to create a salad with big flavors.

As tasty as it is, make sure to leave room for the entree.

Ingredients

  • 1 or 2 heads of romain lettuce
  • Shaved parmesan cheese
  • Croutons
  • Store bought Caesar Salad Dressing

Steps

  1. Rinse, dry, and chop romaine into bite-sized pieces
  2. Sprinkle parmesan into chopped romaine
  3. Add croutons to mix
  4. Drizzle salad dressing onto salad
  5. Mix and enjoy
